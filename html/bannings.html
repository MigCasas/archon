Authorized cards

Players may only use cards as specified in rule 3.3 of the Magic: The Gathering Tournament Rules. However we strongly recommend to allow the use of Gold-bordered cards and square cards, as longthey are not distinguishable from the rest of the deck. This can be achieved by using opaque sleeves for example. That said, he following types of cards should not be used:

  • Silver-borderd cards
  • Conspiracies
  • Oversized and other miscellaneous cards such as Planechase Planes, Archenemy Schemes, Vanguard cards, Helvault cards, etc.
  • Cards that the reference “playing for ante”
  • Physical abilities cards
  • Cards that create subgames
